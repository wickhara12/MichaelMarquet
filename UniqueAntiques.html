Description

The purpose of this unique book is to share his love and passion for collecting antiques by telling a good yarn in a quirky style of writing. Reminiscing and looking back on how Michael obtained many of his beautiful antiques and paintings. And has sometimes been labelled an eccentric collector.

This book contains short stories about some of his unique treasures, which Michael hopes both reader and collector will enjoy.
