Description

Micheal Marquet was one of the 900 million who could not read or write. When he left school at the age of fifteen, the only qualifications he had were years of failing, a reading age of a six-year-old, and an IQ of 70.
He could not even spell his own Name.

This is the remarkable story of the how he overcome his years of failure and went forward with determination to acheive the goals he set himself.

This book recaps on how I overcome the adversity of leaving school at the age of fifteen unable to spell my own name. Over the decade, with sheer grit and determination. I succeeded in completing a horticultural apprenticeship, won a scholarship, and was awarded the pretigious UNESCO Literacy Award which I travelled to Paris to recieve.
