Description

This is a story about one man's endeavour to succeed. This is Michael's personal triumph as a young man, to overcome his speech and language difficulties.

Michael's story gives us a rare opportunity to experience the frustration that comes from having difficulty with speech and language disabilities.
This is a message of hope and endurance for those with learning disabilities and in supporting their families. There are also important messages for teachers and school administrators, which should be heeded closely.

Frist edition printed in February 1988.
Second edition printed in August 1988.
